Open agri innovation and collaboration

FarmStack accelerates data-driven open collaboration in the agri sector.

We build, connect and inspire.

We farmers.

We ignite data- and tech-driven open online/offline collaborations between tech entrepreneurs, farmers and domain experts. We wrangle data, build APIs and prototypes, host hackathons with one goal in mind: better tech for farmers.

"Make things open, it makes them better"

Inspired by Government Digital Service we strive for complete opennes: open data, open source and open collaboration and communication. We believe great things happen when people from various backgrounds collaborate and innovate together out in the open.
